Shaniwar Wada: The Seat of Peshwas

History

Shaniwar Wada was the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire until 1818. Built in 1732 by Peshwa Baji Rao I, it was a seven-story high stone mansion. Although much of the structure was destroyed in a fire in 1828, the massive walls and the famous Delhi Darwaza still stand as a symbol of Maratha pride in the heart of Pune City.

Major Attractions

  • ▶ Delhi Darwaza: The massive gate.
  • ▶ Hazari Karanje: The lotus fountain.
  • ▶ Evening Show: The Light & Sound show.

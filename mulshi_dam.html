Mulshi Dam: The Nature's Paradise

About

Mulshi Dam is formed by the Mula river and is located in the Mulshi taluka of Pune district. It is a major hydroelectric power project in Maharashtra. Surrounded by lush green forests of the Sahyadri ranges, it has become a hotspot for campers, nature lovers and photographers, especially during the monsoon season when the fog covers the entire lake.

Major Attractions

  • ▶ Lake Side Camping: Popular spot for night camping and bonfires.
  • ▶ Tamhini Ghat: A scenic mountain pass nearby with many waterfalls.
  • ▶ Tata Power Plant: A major hydroelectric power station.

Visitor's Guide

Best Time:Monsoon (July to September)
Timings:Open 24 Hours (Sunrise to Sunset preferred)
Distance:45 KM from Pune City

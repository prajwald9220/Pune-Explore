Dagdusheth Ganpati: The Pride of Pune

History

Shrimant Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati is one of the most beloved deities in Maharashtra. The temple was founded in 1893 by Dagdusheth Halwai, a sweet maker, after he lost his son to a plague epidemic. Lokmanya Tilak, a close friend of Dagdusheth, saw the devotion and turned the Ganesh festival into a public celebration to unite people against British rule.

Major Attractions

  • ▶ Golden Idol: The 7.5 feet idol is adorned with nearly 40kg of gold.
  • ▶ Ganesh Chaturthi: A grand 10-day celebration with beautiful decorations.
  • ▶ Daily Aarti: A deeply spiritual experience for thousands of devotees.

Visitor's Guide

Best Time: Morning Aarti (6:00 AM) or Festivals
Timings: 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM
Location: Budhwar Peth, Pune City Center

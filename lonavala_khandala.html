Lonavala & Khandala: The Twin Hill Stations

About

Lonavala and Khandala are twin hill stations in the Sahyadri ranges that demarcate the Konkan coast and the Deccan plateau. Discovered by Lord Elphinstone in 1871, these hill stations are world-famous for their scenic green valleys, misty mountains and the world-famous "Lonavala Chikki". It is the most popular weekend getaway for people from Pune and Mumbai.

Major Attractions

  • ▶ Tiger Point: A cliff top with amazing valley views.
  • ▶ Bhushi Dam: Famous for its waterfalls during monsoon.
  • ▶ Karla & Bhaja Caves: Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves.
